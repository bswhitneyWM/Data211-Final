Country Breakdown of Average Revenue

The next element I investigated was the country's average revenue for a movie. Below is a Tableau dashboard consisting of a choropleth showing a movie's average revenue when produced in each country and a filterable bar graph ranking the countries with the highest average revenue per movie. The interactive dashboard can be found here.

The Tableau dashboard showing each country's average movie revenue. Only countries that produced at least 5 movies were included in the bar graph.

Despite only producing 5 movies, Malta boasted the highest average movie revenue bringing in over $421 million on average. Of the countries that appeared in the top 10 for number of movies produced, only 4 of them also appear in the top 10 for average movie revenue (China, United Kingdom, United States, and Australia). This suggest that quantity doesn't always lead to quality when producing movies.
