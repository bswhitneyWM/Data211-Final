Production Country Breakdown

The first element I investigated was the breakdown of movies produced in each country. Below is a Tableau dashboard consisting of a choropleth showing the number of movies produced in each country and a filterable bar graph ranking the countries with the most movies. The interactive dashboard can be found here.

The Tableau dashboard showing the number of movies produced by each country. If a movie was produced in multiple countries, it is included in the count for both countries.

The most obvious observation is that the United States produces significantly more movies than any other country in this data. 4,301 movies were produced in the United States, while the United Kingdom was second with only 666 movies. Once you get outside the top ten countries, no country produced more than 73 movies. It is also interesting to note that all the top 5 countries come from North America and Europe. It is unclear whether this is due to data availability, cultural views on movies, or some other factor, but could be interesting to explore in the future.
