Country Breakdown

On top of investigating the correlation of movie components with the movie's revenue, I wanted to see if there were any interesting patterns that arose from a country-level analysis of the movies. Do certain countries tend to have more positive reviews than others? Do some countries value quantity over quality and produce lots of movies without bringing a lot of revenue in? Specifically, I looked at the country breakdown of the number of movies produced, a movie's average revenue, and a movie's average score. Movies without data for budget, revenue, or voter scores were not included in this section, leaving 7306 movies to be analyzed.
